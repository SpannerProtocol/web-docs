What is Spanner?

Blockchain projects on steroids

Create an asset, enroll in Spanner DeFi for liquidity and promote them with Growth Templates -- all with little-to-no code. Focus on your users and let Spanner take care of the rest. Use Spanner Components to build Smart Contracts to quickly get your project's application up-and-running.

Spanner Components: DApp building blocks

An expanding catalogue of components

Spanner components are designed to be highly configurable without coding. For the technically savvy, our components are written with extensibility as a core principle. Our components help new projects develop fast, securely and affordably. Bundle them for your Smart Contract and focus on building your DApp.
