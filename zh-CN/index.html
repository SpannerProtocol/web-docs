What is Spanner?

Spanner components are designed to be highly configurable without coding. For the technically savvy, our components are written with extensibility as a core principle. Our components help new projects develop fast, securely and affordably.

Create an asset, enroll in Spanner DeFi for liquidity and promote them with Growth Components -- all with little-to-no code. Focus on your users and let Spanner take care of the rest. Use Spanner Components to build Smart Contracts to quickly get your project's application up-and-running.

BulletTrain: the First Template for Growth

Grow the community for your hot crypto project with the first Spanner Component. Start a BulletTrain on Spanner Blockchain to empower your community to grow itself through affiliate crowdfunding incentives.
