DPO Features

Decentralized

Start working with any like minded individuals around the world. Collaborate with transparency and trust. Measure contributions accurately and reward fairly.

Programmable

Create an organization on Spanner with On-Chain smart contract programming, connect your organization to others via Cross-Chain bridging and Off-Chain oracles.

Organization

Supports collaboration of various size, from a two-man start-up, to a multinational corporation. DPO can grow standalone or grow by cross-DPO merging and collaborations.

DPO vs DAO vs Traditional Organizations

Standing on the shoulder of giants. Building from the best of DAO and Traditional Organizations.

Decentralization provides trust and transparency for open participation.

Rationality provides effectiveness and efficiency for the goals of organizations.
